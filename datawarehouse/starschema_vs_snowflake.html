Star schema and snowflake schema are two popular database schema designs used in data warehousing.
They represent different approaches to organizing data in a relational database, especially when dealing with a large amount of structured data for reporting and analysis.

use Case Star Schema Snowflake Schema
Hierarchies Hierarchies for the dimensions are stored in the dimensional table. Hierarchies are divided into separate tables.
Data structure Denormalized Data structure and query also run faster. Normalized Data Structure.
Query Performance Usually fast because it's straightforward with single join with Fact and Dimensions. Might be a bit slower due to more complicated connections between tables.
Ease of Use Generally easier for business users to understand and navigate due to its simplicity. Can be more complex for business users to understand, especially with multiple levels of normalization.
